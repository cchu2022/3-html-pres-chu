WORLD HUNGER

815 MILLION PEOPLE IN THE WORLD DO NOT HAVE ENOUGH FOOD TO LEAD A HEALTHY ACTIVE LIFE.

WHAT IS THE PROBLEM?

The world produces enough food to feed all 7 billion people, so why are there still people in the world suffering from hunger? The answer is that some people are simply too poor buy food or they lack the resources to grow food. The main cause of world hunger is poverty. People can't afford to buy nutritious food. Because of the economic imbalance and the unequal distribution of food, many people are undernourished, causing them to suffer malntrition.

GLOBALLY,1 IN 9 PEOPLE ARE UNDERNOURISHED, MEANING 12.9% OF THE WORLD'S POPULATION IS HUNGRY.

WHY IS HUNGER A PROBLEM?

Hunger is causing millions of people, locally and worldwide, to have malnutrition. Malnutrition occurs when a person’s diet doesn’t contain the right amount of nutrients they need, causing their body to not function properly. This results in the person being undernourished, leading to the person being more prone to diseases, weak immune systems, physical and mental stunting, and it limits their abilities in life.

POOR NUTRITION CAUSES 45% OF DEATHS IN CHILDREN UNDER THE AGE OF 5.

HOW IS THIS AFFECTING HIGH SCHOOL STUDENTS?

A healthy population is a key piece in building a better future. Having healthy and strong people would lead to more educated people, a better worldwide economy, a decrease in worldwide health issues, as well as a decline in the spread of diseases. More healthy people in the world means more people able to contribute to the growth and development of our world, which will benefit all of us.

HOW TO HELP

World hunger is not a problem that can be solved overnight. It is a long process and involves a lot of money. However, every little thing helps. So what can you do? Be mindful of what you do with your food. Don’t buy more than you need and don’t be wasteful. Make good choices with what you’re doing with the food you have. You can also support local farmers and markets, as well as donate to organizations that are collecting money to send to places all over the world that need financial support for food.

The Global Food Banking Network is an international non-profit organization working towards achieving a hunger-free future. You can join them in their mission of defeating world hunger by donating today.
